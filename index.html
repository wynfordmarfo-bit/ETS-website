ABOUT US

Born From Our Own Journey Through School

Elite Touch Sports was founded with one goal: creating pathways for all students. We leverage our reach within the sports world and beyond to provide the mentorship and industry access that can change the trajectory of a student's life.

We aren't just building athletes we are nurturing the leaders of the next generation. Through a blend of training and immersive workshops, we connect students with world class achievers ranging from Premier League icons to visionary leaders in business, media, and technology.

We bring external opportunities into the school environment, dismantling the barriers of race, religion, gender, income or simply the lack of knowledge on where to find opportunities outside the school building. No student should miss out because of where they come from.

Unlike most sports providers who deliver sessions and move on, we don't just show up and leave. We invest in the whole student their mindset, their ambition, their future. The work we do inside schools is designed to stay with young people long after the final lesson. Creating memories, confidence, and connections that last well beyond their school years. That's what sets us apart.
